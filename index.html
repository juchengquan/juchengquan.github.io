About

I am working as post-doc research fellow in Energy Research Institute, Nanyang Technological University (ERI@N).

My research interest includes energy management, renewable & energy storage planning, hybrid energy storage system, distributed optimization and hierarchical control in microgrids & power systems.

Leveraging techniques from economy evaluation to reliability reinforcement, I have proposed models to aim efficientand reliable operation tasks for microgrids with monitoring & supervisory control algorithms and secured protection schemes.
